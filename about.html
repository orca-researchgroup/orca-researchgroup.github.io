About us

We are a multidisciplinary research group withm embers from diverse countries and scientific backgrounds, united by a shared interest in understanding air-sea interactions. Our work combines in-situ measurements, satellite observations, and numerical simulations to investigate how the ocean, atmosphere, and gravity waves interact across a wide range of spatial and temporal scales, from (sub)mesoscale processes to basin-scale circulation. Through this integrated approach, we aim to advance fundamental knowledge of coupled ocean-atmosphere dynamics and their role in shaping our environment.

Our mission

Our mission is to explore the complex interactions between the ocean, atmosphere, and waves. We bring together researchers from different backgrounds and countries to study these processes across multiple scales. We work to generate knowledge that helps improve environmental prediction, climate understanding, and ocean stewardship.
